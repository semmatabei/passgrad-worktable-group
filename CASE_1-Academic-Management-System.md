Academic Management System

# **Background and Goal**

- Problem: We want to get into a B2B business in the higher-education industry. While we already have a Workflow Management System ("WMS") ready, the feature is not lucrative enough. ISTN, our potential university partner, also mentioned that they do not have an additional budget for our WMS.
- Opportunities:
  - We can get around ISTN's budget limitations if we can takeover one of their vendor positions. One potential vendor that we can take over is Sevima, a vendor for their Academic Management System ("AMS"). The plan is to match their pricing and AMS features, while offering an additional feature: WMS.
  - Unlike WMS, which is a relatively new product and concept in the market, AMS is a more common product in the industry. Having AMS as our product will expand our target market, and get an opportunity to tap into more universities.
- Goal: To launch AMS product in Q2-26
